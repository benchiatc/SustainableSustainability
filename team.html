NOC-SHEN

NUS Overseas College
Shanghai House of ENtrepreneur (SHEN)

The NUS Overseas Colleges (NOC) programme provides NUS students with the entrepreneurial experience needed to kickstart the entrepreneural journey in up to eleven global entrepreneurial hotspots, being at the centre of the entrepreneuship scene and innovative start-ups, while attending courses at our renowned partner universities.
NUS (SHEN) is a youth organization made up of students enrolled into NOC programme in Shanghai. SHEN seeks to raise the level of interest in business and entrepreneurship amongst fellow Singaporeans and Chinese in Shanghai.


OEP

Overseas Entrepreneurship Programme

The Overseas Entrepreneurship Programme (OEP) provides interning Nanyang Technological University, Singapore (NTU Singapore) undergraduates with the experience in a fast-paced startup ecosystem to hone their entrepreneurial skills. In the process, they contribute to building a successful new business.

Strategic Alliance Partner

Singcham Shanghai

Business Association

SingCham Shanghai is a chapter of the Singapore Chamber of Commerce and Industry in China (SingCham), with a mission to champion the interests of Singaporean business community in Shanghai. It is the de-facto platform for business and social activities for Singaporeans, Singaporean companies, and affiliated network in Shanghai. SingCham Shanghai is evolved from Shanghai Singapore Business Association (SSBA) with a 20-year history of serving the Singaporean business community in Shanghai.

Headquartered in Beijing with nine Chapters across China, SingCham is a non-profit organisation supported by the Embassy of the Republic of Singapore to People's Republic of China and Enterprise Singapore, to promote Singapore's interest and expand economic relations between Singapore and China, and to facilitate business and economic exchanges between its members and the relevant Chinese authorities, enterprises and institutions, and amongst Singapore firms with business interests in China..

XNode

Corporate-Startup Innovation Accelerator

Here at XNode, we utilize our unique position as the most international platform in Shanghai to provide high-quality Chinese localization services to startups and entrepreneurs, and connect them to topnotch domestic and international stakeholders.

On top of providing workspaces for entrepreneurs across Shanghai, we are also partnered with 10 incubators and innovation programs from around the world to foster a community where both Chinese and overseas entrepreneurs can exchange their ideas. We are passionate about helping next-generation entrepreneurs re-define, disrupt, and re-establish existing structures in their respective industries.

Because we live and operate within the startup ecosystem, we deeply understand how startups work and connect with them on a daily basis. At the same time, we also understand that the ever-changing nature of the digital world is making innovation more pressing than ever for large corporations who now risk becoming obsolete. To address this issue, we also work to bridge the gap between corporates and startups, foster strong intrapreneurship, and give both corporates and startups a platform for them to each demonstrate what they do best.

We are positioned right at the center of two worlds—embracing the mindsets of both startups and corporations. Join us in our journey to help these two communities connect with one another, learn new innovative strategies, and work together to unleash their maximum potential.

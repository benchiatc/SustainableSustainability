NOC-SHEN

NUS Overseas College
Shanghai House of ENtrepreneur (SHEN)

The NUS Overseas Colleges (NOC) programme provides NUS students with the entrepreneurial experience needed to kickstart the entrepreneural journey in up to eleven global entrepreneurial hotspots, being at the centre of the entrepreneuship scene and innovative start-ups, while attending courses at our renowned partner universities.
NUS (SHEN) is a youth organization made up of students enrolled into NOC programme in Shanghai. SHEN seeks to raise the level of interest in business and entrepreneurship amongst fellow Singaporeans and Chinese in Shanghai.


OEP

Overseas Entrepreneurship Programme

The Overseas Entrepreneurship Programme (OEP) provides interning Nanyang Technological University, Singapore (NTU Singapore) undergraduates with the experience in a fast-paced startup ecosystem to hone their entrepreneurial skills. In the process, they contribute to building a successful new business.

Co-organizing partners

Green Initiatives

Environmental Nonprofit Organization

Green Initiatives is a nonprofit organization that promotes awareness, facilitates actions, implements projects, and stimulates change toward sustainable models of growth and consumption.


Innospace+

Innovation & Entrepreneurship community

INNOSPACE+ is a comprehensive, one-stop solution platform for entrepreneurship. There are a combination of key elements that aim to satisfy the demands of the entrepreneurs in all areas ranging from work, growth, communication and life.

Strategic Alliance Partner

Shanghai Singapore Business Association

Business Association

Shanghai Singapore Business Association (SSBA) is the only comprehensive business platform for the Singaporean community in Shanghai that aims to promote the development and growth of the Singaporean business community in China through diverse business and social events.


Supporting Partners

OSG

Non-profit Social Enterprise

OSG is a nonprofit social enterprise originated from Singapore with a mission of developing Global-minded, China-ready and entrepreneurial Youths. We accomplish this through sharing and networking with experts and practitioners and also through experiential activities like study tours and internships.


The Women's Entrepreneur Lab (TWEL)

Social Enterprise

The Women's Entrepreneur Lab (TWEL) - a social enterprise project, is a cross-border ecosystem for startup, business and career development curated for women and entrepreneurs. At TWEL, you get to work, connect, learn, exchange, experience & grow. Our vision is to provide women the rights and opportunity to own their business and a sustainable lifelong learning, coaching and mentorship. A place to learn, collaborate and achieve your life goals.
